5 Core Demands

THE MANIFESTO.

Five non-negotiable demands for the India every young person deserves.

1
📋

EXAM LEAKS =
NATIONAL BETRAYAL

Students prepare for years. Corruption destroys futures overnight.

  • Non-bailable offences for paper leak perpetrators
  • Lifetime bans for officials involved
  • Fast-track courts for exam fraud
  • End-to-end transparent examination systems
2
🛡️

SAFE CAMPUSES.
SAFE FUTURES.

No student should choose between education and safety.

  • Mandatory campus safety audits
  • Adequate lighting and safe transport
  • 24/7 emergency helplines on every campus
  • Zero-tolerance policy for harassment
3
⚖️

EQUAL RIGHTS.
EQUAL RESPECT.

Equality for all. Respect goes both ways. Mental health matters.

  • Equal pay and opportunity in every field
  • Men's mental health destigmatized
  • Fast justice for gender-based crimes
  • 33% women in leadership — minimum
4
🚫

NO VIP CULTURE.
NO FREEBIE POLITICS.

Leadership is service. Youth want opportunities, not dependency.

  • Jobs over slogans
  • Skills over handouts
  • Accountability over privilege
  • Every public servant answers to the public
5
🏛️

JUSTICE SHOULD NOT
DEPEND ON POWER.

Your surname should not determine your sentence. Politics shouldn't require connections.

  • Legally mandated case resolution timelines
  • No political interference in investigations
  • Equal legal aid for every Indian
  • Elected officials under the same law as everyone

Identity

WHO IS YIP?

YIP was not born in boardrooms by career politicians. It was born out of frustration — in classrooms, college corridors, and late-night conversations about a country that deserves better.

We are students, graduates, first-time voters, and everyday citizens who refused to stay silent.

Other parties talk TO youth. YIP is BUILT BY youth.
