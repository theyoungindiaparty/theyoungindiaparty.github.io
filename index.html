5 Core Demands

THE
MANIFESTO.

Five non-negotiable demands for the India every young person deserves to grow up in.

1
📋

EXAM LEAKS =
NATIONAL BETRAYAL

Students prepare for years. Corruption destroys futures overnight. This ends now.

  • Non-bailable offences for paper leak perpetrators
  • Lifetime bans for officials involved
  • Fast-track courts for exam fraud cases
  • End-to-end transparent examination systems
2
🛡️

SAFE CAMPUSES.
SAFE FUTURES.

No girl should feel unsafe while building her future. No student should have to choose.

  • Mandatory campus safety audits
  • Adequate lighting and safe transport
  • 24/7 emergency helplines on every campus
  • Zero-tolerance policy for harassment
3
⚖️

EQUAL RIGHTS.
EQUAL RESPECT.

Equality for all — women and men. Respect should go both ways. Men's mental health matters too.

  • Equal pay and opportunity in every field
  • Men's mental health destigmatized
  • Fast justice for gender-based crimes
  • 33% women in leadership — minimum
4
🚫

NO VIP CULTURE.
NO FREEBIE POLITICS.

Leadership is service, not privilege. Youth want opportunities, not dependency.

  • Jobs over slogans
  • Skills over handouts
  • Accountability over privilege
  • Every public servant answers to the public
5
🏛️

JUSTICE SHOULD NOT
DEPEND ON POWER.

Your surname should not determine your sentence. Politics should not require powerful connections.

  • Legally mandated case resolution timelines
  • No political interference in investigations
  • Equal legal aid for every Indian
  • Elected officials under the same law as everyone

Identity

WHO IS YIP?

YIP was not born in boardrooms by career politicians. It was born out of frustration — in classrooms, college corridors, and late-night conversations about a country that deserves better.

Other parties talk TO youth. YIP is BUILT BY youth.

Education Reform

Systemic overhaul of how India teaches its children — from rote learning to real thinking.

Campus Safety

Every student deserves a campus that feels like a safe place to grow, not a place to survive.

Equal Rights

Equality is not a favour extended to some — it is the baseline every Indian deserves.

Real Opportunities

Jobs, skills, and a future that doesn't depend on who your father knows or what party you belong to.

Transparent Justice

A legal system where power doesn't determine outcome, and every case moves forward fairly.

India 2047+

We are building for 100 years, not the next election cycle. Future first, always.

IF NOT US, WHO?
The youth of India has waited long enough. We are the generation that will finally act.
IF NOT NOW, WHEN?
The time to act is not tomorrow. It is not after the next election. It is today.
IF NOT HERE, WHERE?
This is our country. We inherited its problems. We will shape its future.
